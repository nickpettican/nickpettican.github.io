Dharmabot Privacy Policy

This chatbot privacy policy describes how Dharmabot protects and makes use of the information you give Dharmabot when you use this chatbot.

If you are asked to provide information when using this chatbot, it will only be used in the ways described in this privacy policy.

This policy is updated from time to time. The latest version is published on this page.

This chatbot privacy policy was updated on: 17th June 2018

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact Nicolas Pettican.

Introduction

We gather and use certain information about individuals in order to provide products and services and to enable certain functions on this chatbot.

We also collect information to better understand how visitors use this chatbot and to present timely, relevant information to them.

What data we gather

We may collect the following information:

  • Name and gender.
  • Demographic information, such as locale and timezone.
  • Chatbot usage data.

How we use this data

Collecting this data helps us understand what you are looking from Dharmabot, enabling us to deliver an improved service.

Specifically, we may use data:

  • To customise the chatbot for you.
  • To improve the service.
  • To ensure the messages adhere to your time of day.

Controlling information about you

We will never lease, distribute or sell your personal information to third parties unless we have your permission or the law requires us to.

Any personal information we hold about you is stored and processed under our data protection policy, in line with the Data Protection Act 1998.

Security

We will always hold your information securely. To prevent unauthorised disclosure or access to your information, we have implemented strong physical and electronic security safeguards.

We also follow stringent procedures to ensure we work with all personal data in line with the Data Protection Act 1998.

How to gain access to it

If any point you want us to remove your data, contact Nicolas Pettican and we will give you a subject access request form to fill out.

Links from our site

Our chatbot may contain links to other websites.

Please note that we have no control of websites outside the facebook.com & messenger.com domains. If you provide information to a website to which we link, we are not responsible for its protection and privacy.

Always be wary when submitting data to websites. Read the site’s data protection and privacy policies fully.
