## What I'm doing now

### Building

Offering Bowl — a platform that lets lay sponsors support Buddhist
monastics directly. I founded it, designed it, and write most of the
code. Currently in private beta with several monasteries.

### Studying

Madhyamaka philosophy at the Dzongsar Khyentse Chökyi Lodrö Institute
in Bir, Himachal Pradesh, alongside academic research affiliated with
Savitribai Phule Pune University.

### Practising

Monastic life. Fully ordained (Bhikshu) in the Tibetan tradition,
living at the monastery year-round.

## Selected work

**Offering Bowl** (current) — Founder and lead engineer of a sponsorship
