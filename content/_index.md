## What I'm doing now

### Building

Online platforms and initiatives to support the longevity of Buddhism.
Currently leading the development of Offering Bowl — a platform that lets
lay sponsors support monastics directly.

### Studying

Advanced Buddhist philosophy at the Dzongsar Khyentse Chökyi Lodrö Institute
in Bir, Himachal Pradesh, alongside academic research affiliated with
Savitribai Phule Pune University.

### Practising

Monastic life. Fully ordained (Bhikshu) in the Mūlasarvāstivāda (Tibetan)
tradition, living at the monastery year-round, unless I am in Pune or in a
forest monastery in Thailand.
